### ✨ Features

- ✅ **4 Data Sources:** CRM (Zoho), Support Tickets, Emails, Slack notes
- ✅ **25 Customers** across 15 industries
- ✅ **Automated Risk Detection** — 🟢 Healthy / 🟡 Needs Attention / 🔴 At Risk
- ✅ **Signal Analysis** — urgent tickets, negative sentiment, expansion opportunities
- ✅ **Next Best Action** — prioritized recommendations with reasoning
- ✅ **Pipeline Overview** — instant snapshot of all customers
- ✅ **Export as PDF** — save customer views for reporting
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** — press `Esc` to close panels

## 📊 Task 3: Spreadsheet Dashboard Challenge

**🎯 Target:** Help a sales team manage its lead pipeline and answer 8 key operational questions.

### 🔗 Links

| Resource | Link |
|----------|------|
| **📄 Sample Data** | [lead_pipeline_data.csv](./task-3-spreadsheet/lead_pipeline_data.csv) — 35 leads |
| **📖 Setup Guide** | [dashboard_explanation.md](./task-3-spreadsheet/dashboard_explanation.md) |

### 📈 Data Snapshot

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Leads | 35 |
| Lead Channels | 8 (Website, Referral, LinkedIn, Email, Conference, Google Ads, Podcast, YouTube) |
| Team Members | 4 (Priya, Rahul, Neha, Ankit) |
| Pipeline Stages | 6 (Discovery → Qualified → Proposal Sent → Negotiation → Closed Won → Closed Lost) |
| Industries | 15+ (SaaS, FinTech, Healthcare, Biotech, CleanTech, AI/ML, Media, Logistics, etc.) |

### ✅ Answers to All 8 Dashboard Questions

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | How many leads came in? | 35 leads tracked with dates |
| 2 | Where did leads come from? | 8 channels with revenue attribution |
| 3 | Which leads are still open? | Filter by stage ≠ Closed Won/Lost |
| 4 | Which leads need follow-up? | Conditional formatting on Follow-Up Date |
| 5 | Which channels perform best? | Channel Performance query (volume + value) |
| 6 | Where are leads getting stuck? | Stage-wise bottleneck analysis |
| 7 | Which opportunities are high priority? | High-value + High-priority filter |
| 8 | What actions should the team take? | Overdue follow-up list + stuck lead alerts |
